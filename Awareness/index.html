Our Mission

Our goal is to make a single website that will hold all of the information you need to become aware about the issues that plague our time.

Education is a communal objective, which is why we've created a way to share useful videos, websites, and information in our user-driven forum. In addition, check out some of the games on the website and use our interactive tool that we hope will spread awareness on all topics, of all kinds.

Who Are We

We are a group of high school students who want to spread awareness about the social issues of today, while also combatting fake news and misinformation, which are detrimental to the health of our society.
